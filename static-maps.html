Static Maps

These are some random - and less random - static maps.


Circulation of the Evansville Courier & Press

The Courier & Press is Evansville's daily newspaper and is distributed in 30 counties of the tri-state area.





2010 New York State Election

Democrat Governor Cuomo won the 2010 Elections in New York State. In the map are the vote margins of Cuomo and the Republican candidate at the time, Carl Paladino.

Migration routes

This map represents migration routes, major or minor depending on the thickness of the line. The original idea comes from this Reuters article: Europe's migration crisis.
